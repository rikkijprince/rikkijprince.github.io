## Privacy Policy

This website is operated by **Rikki J Prince**.

Your privacy is important. This page explains what data is collected and how it is used.

---

### 1. Personal Data

This website does not require users to create accounts and does not knowingly collect personal data such as names, addresses, or email addresses.

---

### 2. Analytics

This website uses **Google Analytics** to understand how visitors use the site.

The data collected may include:
- pages visited
- time spent on pages
- general geographic location (country or region)
- device and browser type

IP addresses are anonymized before storage.

This data is used solely to improve website content and user experience.

---

### 3. Cookies

Google Analytics may place cookies on your device to distinguish unique visitors and sessions.

These cookies are used only for statistical purposes and do not enable personal identification.

---

### 4. Third-Party Services

This site may contain links to third-party websites (such as Amazon or external retailers).  
This privacy policy applies only to this website.

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

To exercise these rights, you may contact the site operator.
